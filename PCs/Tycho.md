# Backstory


Tycho is a 319 year old gnome wizard/artificer who is a formerly retired adventurer just getting back into the action.

Tycho lost his party when encountering an Ancient Green Dragon and he was just able to teleport away with his life. Traumatized by this narrow escape, he swore off adventuring as he never wanted to lose anyone again.

For the last 100 years he owned and operated Tycho's Tantalizing Trickets and Other Tomfoolery in Neverwinter. He sells toys for children but his crowing achievement is his "moving painting", essentially a permanent moving photograph crafted by permanent (4th level+) castings of the Major Image spell.

Business has not gone well as everything is exorbitantly expensive to produce and the Zhentarim have exploited this, driving Tycho into a deep debt. They have taken control business license and are demanding protection fees that Tycho is unable to pay. Tycho suspects that perhaps the Zhantarim may be using his inventions and illusions for an ulterior motive (e.g. propaganda, weapons, etc.). Driven by financial ruin, Tycho returned to adventuring to make ends meet with the ultimate goal of paying off his debt and reopening the business.

To his own surprise, Tycho has fallen in love once again with adventuring even with the aches and pains he has now in his old age that he didn't have in his youth. He has traveled to the Frigid North in hopes of finding a profitable adventure and make a dent against his debt.
